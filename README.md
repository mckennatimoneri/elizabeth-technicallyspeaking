## 💼 My Skills
I have expertise in web development, proficiently working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create engaging websites. My skills extend to utilizing tools like Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics for tracking and optimizing web performance, and VWO for A/B testing and conversion optimization.

In the realm of digital marketing, I excel at SEO, harnessing the power of BigQuery for data analysis, and leveraging VWO for marketing insights. I am well-versed in managing digital paid media platforms and adept at using social media to drive brand engagement.

My data science capabilities include applying media mix modeling, forecasting, and linear regression to derive actionable insights. I perform sentiment analysis to gauge public opinion and conduct media opportunity analysis to maximize marketing impact.

On the creative front, I am skilled in using Adobe Creative Suite and Figma for designing visually appealing graphics and user interfaces. Additionally, I employ MapBox for creating interactive maps and visualizations.

This blend of technical, analytical, and creative skills allows me to tackle diverse projects with a comprehensive approach. 🚀
